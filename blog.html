The Life and Teachings of Jesus

Jesus of Nazareth, also known as Jesus Christ, is the central figure of Christianity. Born over 2000 years ago, his teachings, life, and crucifixion have profoundly shaped human history.

Christians believe Jesus is the Son of God and the awaited Messiah prophesied in the Old Testament. His teachings emphasized love, compassion, forgiveness, and the importance of faith.

Parables and miracles recorded in the Gospels illustrate moral lessons and spiritual truths. Beyond religious texts, Jesus remains a significant historical figure, whose philosophy of love and humility has influenced cultures, ethics, art, and society worldwide.
